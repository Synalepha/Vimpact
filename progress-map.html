What this map is—and is not

A starting framework, open to correction.

The map is a selective editorial interpretation, not a comprehensive history or a claim that progress has been continuous. Dates identify a representative publication, action, law, or period—not the beginning or end of a struggle.

Future editions should be reviewed by historians, movement participants, policy specialists, and communities whose work is represented. They should add primary sources, contested interpretations, global perspectives, and a public correction process.
