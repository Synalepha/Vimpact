Action Agenda - Conscious Architects

Status: Founder’s Draft 1.0. This is a proposal, not a member-approved platform.

Process status: V-Impact has not yet published amendment, voting, leadership-accountability, or decision-record rules.

The Action Agenda is where V-Impact moves from vision to implementation. Grounded in the Platform of Conscious Progress, this agenda outlines the strategic steps we are taking to build a broad, values-driven voting bloc—one capable of influencing elections, shaping policy, and advancing real solutions at every level of government.

Our aim is not just to resist destructive politics, but to organize around a bold, forward-thinking alternative that reflects the will and well-being of the people.

Voting Bloc

We are creating a movement, in the form of a Voting Bloc, united by the Vision of Conscious Progress and the Mission of Multi-Metric Capitalism. We shall harness the collective power of voters to act with a unified, intentional voice. We refer to members of the Voting Bloc as Conscious Architects (Architects) of the Future.

The establishment of our Voting Bloc serves as a conduit. At its core, it functions as an influential mechanism propelling the Platform of Conscious Progress within the political sphere.

We believe that coming together, outside the quagmire of dual political party failure, provides a sorely needed environment capable of transforming division into unity. We boldly intend to turn the seemingly impossible into the possible and to redefine the boundaries of what we can imagine and achieve in a post Trump future.

The Platform of Conscious Progress
(Founder's Draft 1.0)

Building Wealth and Well-Being

We are implementing a collaborative process for the Architects to evolve the concepts of Conscious Progress and Multi-Metric Capitalism into a comprehensive policy-focused platform, called The Platform of Conscious Progress.

📄 Read the Platform Draft v1.0 (PDF)

It is our intention that The Platform of Conscious Progress will serve as the political platform for politicians in office or running for office. V-Impact will establish Web services both for politicians and their constituents.

Join the Work

We invite you to become a Member of V-Impact’s growing Voting Bloc for Conscious Progress. As members, we share a bold mission: to help transform free-market capitalism into a conscious, regenerative force that truly serves both people and planet.

Our strategy begins with refining and expanding the Platform for Conscious Progress (Founder's Draft 1.0) into a fully developed political platform—one compelling enough to attract aligned candidates and powerful enough to unite voters ready to shape a better future.

Membership is free. As an Architect you will be welcomed to participate in the activities of the Voting Bloc, such as:

  • Refine and Expand upon the Platform of Conscious Progress through Impact Cafes for Platform Development and Forum Discussions for Platform Advancement.
  • Join Voting Bloc activities such as Impact Cafe for Bloc Development and a wide variety of Forum Discussion topics.
  • Participate in outreach by inviting people to visit our site and attend Open Impact Cafes.

Governance still to be built

A voting bloc needs visible democratic rules. V-Impact’s next public governance document should define who can propose platform changes, how disagreement is moderated, how decisions are recorded, who stewards funds and data, and how leaders are selected and held accountable.

Until those rules are published, this site describes the platform as a founder’s draft and participation as an invitation to help shape the work—not as proof of member approval.
