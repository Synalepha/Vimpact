Contact & site status.

Vimpact.org does not currently offer direct contact or private feedback. The available channel is V-Impact’s external Substack.

How to reach the project today: Read and reply through V-Impact’s Substack. Substack applies its own account, privacy, and accessibility practices.

Not available: A monitored organizational email, support channel, contact form, local account, or private feedback service.

What must come before a contact form

V-Impact should first publish who receives messages, expected response times, data retention rules, accessibility and language support, a privacy notice, and an option for people who do not use Substack.
