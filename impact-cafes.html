Impact Café - Shining A Light On A New Way Forward

An Impact Café is a welcoming space—online or in-person—where people come together to explore big ideas, share bold visions, and collaborate on creating a post trump future. It's more than just conversation; it's about meaningful dialogue that leads to real action, grounded in the values of Conscious Progress.

Whether you're dreaming up community solutions, rethinking systems, or just connecting with others who care, the Impact Café is where ideas spark impact.
