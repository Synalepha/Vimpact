The mechanism

Ideas matter when people can shape them.

Conscious Progress means advancing society and technology deliberately—measuring success by shared well-being, democratic power, and ecological health rather than growth alone. V-Impact’s proposed process is a public loop, not a finished answer.

01 · LearnRead the founder’s draft and see which ideas are established, proposed, or still open.
02 · DeliberateUse Impact Cafés and forums to surface lived experience, disagreement, and better proposals.
03 · ReviseDevelop the platform through a documented process. Governance rules and revision history still need to be published.
04 · OrganizeBuild a voting bloc capable of acting together while preserving debate and member agency.
05 · MeasureTrack decisions and outcomes against human, democratic, economic, and ecological goals.
06 · Learn againUse results and community feedback to improve the next version.

The work in front of us

From broad vision to material commitments.

The current Platform of Conscious Progress is a founder’s draft, not a member-approved platform. It proposes a shift toward “multi-metric capitalism,” but key positions remain to be made concrete through accountable public work.

Work and economic powerWages, unions, worker voice, ownership, monopoly power, public goods, and shared prosperity.
Care and human dignityHealthcare, housing, education, disability access, safety, and the conditions for a life with agency.
Racial and gender justiceStructural inequality, wealth gaps, migration, incarceration, bodily autonomy, and equal political power.
Climate and living systemsRegeneration, climate justice, interdependence, durable infrastructure, and responsibility across generations.
Democratic safeguardsConstitutional rights, due process, accountable institutions, fair representation, and resistance to authoritarian power.
Open questionsWho decides, how amendments work, and how leaders and funding are held accountable must be publicly defined.

Accountability before scale

What exists—and what does not yet.

Available now: Founder’s Draft 1.0, a free Substack membership path, an externally hosted forum, the V-Impact story, and this public website.

Not yet published: member-approved platform positions, governance and amendment rules, public impact metrics, a meeting calendar, financial reporting, multilingual editions, or a locally operated membership system.

Our standard: the site will distinguish proposals from decisions and working services from future plans. No participation count or result is claimed without evidence.
