The proposed method

Ideas should become accountable public work.

Conscious Progress asks whether public choices improve shared well-being, democratic agency, economic security, and the health of living systems—not simply whether an economy grows.

The process below is the project’s intended direction. The rules required to operate it have not yet been published.

  1. LearnRead the draft, test its assumptions, and identify what remains unanswered.
  2. DeliberateBring lived experience, evidence, and disagreement into a respectful public conversation.
  3. ReviseDocument proposals, rationales, and changes instead of silently rewriting the record.
  4. OrganizeBuild collective capacity while preserving individual agency and open debate.
  5. MeasureDefine outcomes for people, democracy, the economy, and living systems.
  6. Learn againUse the evidence to improve the next version and make consequences visible.
