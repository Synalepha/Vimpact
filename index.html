The proposed method

Ideas should become accountable public work.

Conscious Progress asks whether public choices improve shared well-being, democratic agency, economic security, and the health of living systems—not simply whether an economy grows.

The process below is the project’s intended direction. The rules required to operate it have not yet been published.

  1. Understand the current draftRead its proposals, test its assumptions, and identify what remains unanswered.
  2. Discuss evidence and experienceBring lived experience, research, and disagreement into a respectful public conversation.
  3. Publish proposed changesDocument each proposal and its rationale instead of silently rewriting the record.
  4. Build collective capacityCoordinate action while preserving individual agency and open debate.
  5. Define success before actingSet outcomes for people, democracy, the economy, and living systems.
  6. Revise based on resultsUse evidence to improve the next version and make consequences visible.

Project status, without the spin.

Rules people need before participating

V-Impact still owes visitors an organizational contact, governance proposal, privacy notice, participation standards, and event host guidance.

Do not assume these exist

Systems that have not been established

There is no member-approved platform, formal voting bloc, published decision process, impact reporting, financial reporting, or Vimpact.org account system.
